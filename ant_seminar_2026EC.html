2026 Spring Elliptic Curves

Course Description

This course covers some basic facts about elliptic curves on various kinds of fields and some key theorems like Mordell-Weil. The definition of L functions will also be included. Students will just be required to attend the lectures and finish two take-homes.

Course Content

Prerequisites

(1)Undergraduate abstract algebra.

(2)Basic analysis, especially for complex analysis.

(3)Basic number theory and classical algebraic geometry(in the words of variety).
